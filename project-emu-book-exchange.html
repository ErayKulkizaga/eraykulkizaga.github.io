EMU Book Exchange

Campus marketplace connecting students for book sales and swaps.

Project Aim

This project aims to solve the problem of high book costs for university students. The platform allows students to easily buy, sell, or exchange second-hand books with fellow students on campus.

The main objectives of the project are:

  • To reduce students' book expenses.
  • To encourage the reuse of books.
  • To create a helpful academic community.
  • To create a platform that enables university students to buy, sell, or exchange books easily and securely.
